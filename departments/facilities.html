How Facilities uses Notion

Room Booking & Space Database

Who has which room, when, and for what — no more double-bookings
▼

A Notion database for room bookings gives every team visibility over space availability. Each booking entry includes: room, date, time, who's using it, purpose, setup needed, and any special requirements. Switch to Calendar view and you can see every booking across the building in one glance.

No more "I thought you had the hall!" moments. No more booking requests buried in WhatsApp. Everything is in one place, visible to everyone, and always current.

💡 Add a Notion form so teams can submit room booking requests themselves. It goes straight into your database for you to confirm — no back-and-forth needed.

Maintenance Request Tracker

Every reported issue tracked through to resolution
▼

A maintenance database captures every reported issue — a broken chair, a leaking tap, a faulty projector, a heating problem. Each entry has: what the issue is, where it is, who reported it, priority level, assigned contractor (if needed), and status.

Staff can submit issues themselves using a simple form. You can see everything open at a glance, prioritise urgent items, and have a full history of every repair ever made.

What this looks like in Notion
1 New page — add a row / entry to your database
2 Add property — add a column (Date, Status, Person…)
3 Add view — switch to Board, Calendar, Gallery & more
4 New + Settings — create entries & configure filters/sorts

📦

Building Asset Inventory

Every piece of equipment, its condition, and when it was last serviced
▼

From the boiler to the fold-up tables, from the projector to the coffee machine — a Notion asset database tracks every physical item in the building. Each entry has: location, purchase date, last serviced, warranty expiry, supplier, and condition.

When something needs replacing, you have the full history at your fingertips — not buried in an old email or a spreadsheet you can't find.

💡 Add a photo of each piece of equipment to its Notion entry. When a contractor or new team member needs to find something, they know exactly what they're looking for.
👷

Supplier & Contractor Directory

Every trusted supplier and contractor — one searchable database
▼

A contacts database for all the people and organisations that keep the building running: cleaners, electricians, plumbers, decorators, security, catering suppliers. Each entry has their contact details, what they cover, typical response time, cost, and any contract details.

When something breaks at 7am on a Sunday, you're not searching through old emails for a phone number. It's in Notion, searchable in seconds.

💡 Link supplier contacts to the maintenance requests they've handled. Over time you can see which contractors are responsive and which take three follow-up calls to arrive.
🔒

Health & Safety Document Hub

Risk assessments, fire plans, and procedures — always accessible and current
▼

H&S documentation needs to be findable, current, and accessible to the right people — not buried in a filing cabinet or a folder on someone's desktop. Notion gives each document a home, with a clear last-reviewed date and owner.

Fire evacuation plans, risk assessments for events, first aid procedures, COSHH documents — all categorised, linked, and searchable. When an inspector or a new volunteer needs something, it takes seconds to find.

🔒 H&S Documentation
📄 Fire Evacuation Plan — reviewed Jan 2026 Current
📄 General Risk Assessment — events Current
📄 First Aid Provision Policy Due review
📄 COSHH register — cleaning products Current
💡 Set a reminder property on each document for its review date. Notion can notify you when a document is due for update — so nothing drifts out of compliance.
🗓

Cleaning & Maintenance Schedules

Recurring tasks that never get forgotten
▼

Create a recurring task database for all scheduled maintenance and cleaning — daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, and annual tasks. Each task has a due date, assignee, and completion status. When one cycle completes, the next is automatically created.

Cleaners and maintenance staff can tick tasks off on their phones. You can see at a glance what's been done today and what's still outstanding — without having to be physically present.

💡 Print a QR code for each room that links to a Notion checklist for that room's cleaning/maintenance tasks. Staff scan it, complete the list, and you see it in real time.
